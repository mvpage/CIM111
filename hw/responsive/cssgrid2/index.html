The Best Student Spaces in Coral Gables
A student centered directory

Cafes Museums Parks & Beaches

Cafes

Small Tea

Small Tea is the perfect place to study if you are looking for a dim and cozy, yet chic teahouse with over 84 tea flavors, decadent pastries, and savory/healthy meal options.

Books & Books

If you love books, or just bookstores in general, Books&Books has an eclectic atmosphere with a variety of unique books. Equipped with an indoor cafe and a courtyard, making it the perfect spot to relax or study.

House of Per'La

If you're looking for a pick-me-up go to House of Per'La for a great new specialty coffee serving healthy options all day.

Cafe Demetrio

With its traditional Italian Fortress archetecture and atmosphere, Cafe Demetrio is a perfect place for a lively meeting or to study on a rainy day. Make sure to try the belgain waffles and french toast!

Museums

Institute of Contemporary Art

If you feel like honing into your abstract/creative side, come over to ICA for a variety of contemporary art. They host fun events year round in their garden, like their annual drag show, and provide student discounts.

Lowe Art Museum

Located at the Univeristy of Miami, the Lowe Art Museum is a visual art museum that offers student discounts year round and also hosts seasonal events and parties.

Coral Gables Museum

The Coral Gables Museum is a city museum housed in a vintage coral-rock buliding; the museum featueres gallery exhibits & a public plaza. Make sure to show your student ID for discounts!

Perez Art Museum

Take the metrorail to Brickell or a quick Uber ride to the contemporary Perez Art Museum to take iconic pictures in their sculpture garden or at the waterfront.

Parks & Beaches

Matheseon Hammock Park

A 30 minute bike ride from UM, Hammock Park is a great place for a small and fast getaway. They offer watersports, pinic areas, and even a shallow inlet for those whose swimming isn't their forte. Come here at 5pm to see a sunset you can't miss.

Fairchild Tropical Botanic Garden

Fairchild Tropical Botanic Garden is an 83 acre botanic garden with extensive collections of rare tropical plants including palms, cycads, and lots of flowers. Come here to relax or for your next photoshoot!

Peacock Park

A quick uber ride from UM, Peacock Park is a 9.4 acre public, urban park in Coconut Grove neighborhood of Miami on the shore of Biscayne Bay.

The Venetian Pool

Cool off on those hot,humid Miami days at the Venetian Pool, a historic swimming pool located in Coral gables for just $20.
